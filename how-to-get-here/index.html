How to get here

Fly

LaGuardia is about 15-25 minutes by car from downtown Brooklyn. JFK is about 35-45 minutes, which is honestly confusing since JFK is in Brooklyn and LaGuardia is in Queens, but here we are.

Amtrak

Do you and Joe Biden share a love for trains? Or are you coming from Boston?

Either way, the train to New York is beautiful and super easy. From Penn Station, a Lyft/Uber/taxi is about 30-45 minutes depending on traffic, and the subway is about 25 minutes.

Drive

On weekends, there is usually street parking if you need it. There are also several garages around downtown Brooklyn including at most of the hotels we recommend.
