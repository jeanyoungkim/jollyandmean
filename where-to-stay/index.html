Where to stay

We’re biased but we think this is the best neighborhood in the city. The hotels below will make it super easy to walk around and explore - but if you want to stay in another part of Brooklyn or in Manhattan, you’ll still only be a 15-30 minute car or subway ride away from the wedding festivities!


NuHotel

Somewhat “hip” and located in downtown Brooklyn, this is a very walkable area near some of our favorite spots.

We have a room block here and rates are $299-349 on Friday night and $369-419 on Saturday night.

You can book online through this link or by phone with this info:

  • Group name: McGrath-Kim Wedding
  • Telephone: 855-752-2010 (Dial 3)

Please note that this block is available until September 1.

Another note: We’ll have a shuttle between this hotel and the wedding venue on Saturday!


Hilton

Same area as NuHotel

Rates start at $369 per night


Even Hotel

A wellness-focused hotel (each room has workout equipment?!) that’s a couple blocks from NuHotel and the Hilton!

Rates start at $270 per night


Fairfield Inn & Suites

This option is in Gowanus and super close to some great breweries and restaurants. Rates start at $250 per night


AirBnb

If you don’t want to go the hotel route, there are a bunch of Airbnbs in this area that are great.

We’d recommend staying in the neighborhoods shaded in green if you're looking to walk around and explore during your down time. The yellow star is where the wedding is and the red star is where we live, as an FYI. If you’re browsing and want a second opinion, Molly could not be more excited to help!
