Where to stay

We’re biased, but we think this is the best neighborhood in the city, so any of the options below will make it super easy to walk around and explore.

That said, if you want to stay in Manhattan or another part of Brooklyn, you absolutely can and you’ll still be a quick 15-30 minute car or subway ride away from the wedding.


NuHotel

“Hip” and located in downtown Brooklyn, this is a very walkable area near some of our favorite spots.

Rates in our block are $299-349 on Friday and $369-419 on Saturday


Hilton

Same area as NuHotel

Rates start at $400 per night


Even Hotel

A wellness-focused hotel (each room has workout equipment?!) that’s a couple blocks from NuHotel and the Hilton!

Rates start at $270 per night


AirBnb

If you don’t want to go the hotel route, there are a bunch of Airbnbs in this area that are great.

We’d recommend staying in the neighborhoods shaded in green if you're looking to walk around and explore during your down time. The yellow star is where the wedding is and the red star is where we live, as an FYI. If you’re browsing and want a second opinion, Molly could not be more excited to help!
